HEART OF THE INDIAN OCEAN

Welcome to Sri lanka

Imagine long stretches of sandy beach flanked by palm trees, slow-moving elephants, and colorfully dressed women picking tea while gossiping about their daily lives; Sri Lanka (previously Ceylon) is just that. Serendib derives the word serendipity, which means 'pleasant and unexpected discovery,' and there is no better way to characterize this lovely country. Friendly people, cultural treasures, and interesting temples, not to mention incredible animals, await you.

Why is it important for a tourist to be healthy?

Travel promotes physical activity, whether it is running through the airport, walking the streets of a city, or climbing a mountain. Physical movement lowers blood pressure and the risk of heart disease. Travel promotes brain health and keeps the mind sharp.


NEWS tourism


What is the current state of tourism in Sri Lanka?

Inbound visitor visits in Sri Lanka totaled 1.9 million in 2019, down 1.8 percent from the previous year. Tourism was the country's third greatest source of foreign cash in 2019, with official tourist revenues estimated at $3.6 billion, down from $4.3 billion in 2018.

Is Sri Lanka now open to tourists?

You certainly can. Foreign tourists are not prohibited in the country. However, make contact with your hotel before boarding the plane.


SL Destination


The island nation of Sri Lanka is nothing short of stunning, with lovely vistas, stirring mountains, luscious green tea fields, and golden beaches. The country is full of breathtaking natural, historic, and cultural sights, each more picture-perfect than the last. Sri Lanka has all you need for a wonderful vacation, from spiritual Buddhist monasteries and temples to unusual and pristine locations rarely visited. Sri Lanka will not fail you, whether you are traveling with your significant other or planning a vacation with your family!
